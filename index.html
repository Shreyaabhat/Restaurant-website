Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi As the name implies, Bella Vista boasts beautiful views, but it is certainly not the only attraction to our lakeside restaurant. Our friendly staff, accompanied by our decadent food and wine served on the terrace, all provide a ‘bellisima’ experience.
